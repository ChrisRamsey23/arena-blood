📜 HOW TO FIGHT

DISTANCE SYSTEM

Combat starts at 10 ft. Advance to close gap (-10 ft). Back Up to retreat (+10 ft). Attack requires 0 ft. Ranged works at >0 ft with a ranged weapon equipped.

ROMAN WEEK

Each fight is a day: Lunae → Martis → Mercurii → Iovis → Veneris → ⭐ Saturni Night. The 6th fight is a Prize Fight with a champion gladiator and bonus rewards.

MERCY

When HP drops to ≤20%, the Mercy! button unlocks. 80% chance the Emperor grants mercy. 20% chance of instant execution. Desperate gamble!

ECONOMY

Earn gold from victories. Heal and buy gear after each fight. Sell old gear for 10% value. Unequip items before selling.
